Orchids
About this beautiful flowers
Orchids grow on every continent except Antarctica, where they can not adapt because of the cold.
In general, these flowers are very fond of humidity and heat - so most grow in countries near the
equator. For example, a lot of orchids growing in Thailand and Malaysia. Often there creating
parks with orchids, in which grow only these pretty flowers.
Now orchid is one of the most beautiful flowers in the world. For example, Panama has chosen him
as their national flower, and for good reason!

Orchids come in a variety of colors: they can be pink, yellow, blue, white, purple and even green.
