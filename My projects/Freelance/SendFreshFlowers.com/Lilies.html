Fresh Lilies
Lily - the flower of the amazing beauty, one of the oldest among the many bulbous plants. There is nothing strange in the fact that lily is now the queen of many gardens, and the image of snow-white lilies, one of the most exquisite lilies, were found in the old manuscripts and ancient frescoes.
Lily bulbs so would have been unknown in Europe, if not the Crusaders who brought them together with a number of other bulbous plants. It took some time, and in the Renaissance white lilies have become a symbol of purity and virginity, which is why they can be seen near the image of the Virgin Mary.
