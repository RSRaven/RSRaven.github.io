Your Guide in Bitcoin World...
Bitcoin (Bitcoin) - is a completely new electronic cash and it is fundamentally different from all other existing electronic currencies and payment systems. The difference is that Bitcoin is not a control and processing centers - all operations take place in the p2p-network.

So what is it, Bitcoin? Bitcoin Virtual Money - is a new cryptocurrency, which created an unknown programmer (or a group of programmers) under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto. This happened in 2009. The term "Bitcoin" and specificity (how it works) invented by him. Nobody knows where this man is and what his name is in the real world. He created not only the program but also a special application - Bitcoin wallet on your PC.

Bitcoin has the basic functions and properties of conventional money from different countries:
- A means of exchange;
- A mesns of savings;
- The unit of account.

However, Bitcoin is cryptocurrency.

Cryptocurrency is a type of digital currency. Its emissions and accounting based on different cryptographic methods. A decentralized operation occurs in a distributed computer network.

It should at least briefly mention the second most popular cryptocurrency - Litecoin. This is the same cryptocurrency only transaction of this cryptocurrency algorithm  slightly faster. Bitcoin and Litecoin can be compared to gold and silver. Today there are already more than a dozen smaller cryptocurrencies.
